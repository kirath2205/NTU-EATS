ABOUT US

Curious to know where to eat? Have a budget to maintain, at the same time have a cusine in mind?
Well,you have come to the right place!
NTU EATS is a NTU food and restaurant recommendation system, used for helping students 
and staff discover great places to eat around them.
The system provides a list of food joints on campus,with their respective details.Besides, it also
allows you to sort the canteens based on your preferences.
